Mead Breakfast

Start the day the way you mead to go on

Odin's preferred way to break his fast

Ingredients

  • Mead
  • optional extra: a little drizzle of honey

Steps

  1. Pour some mead in a bowl
  2. Eat it with a spoon
  3. Odin thinks you are a bit of a fool if you are thinking of adding honey to your mead, "why would you add honey to your honey wine"
