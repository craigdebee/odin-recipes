Mead

The original, and Odin's Fave

Odin reckons you can't go wrong with this one, and he should know, this is literally all he drinks (or eats)

Ingredients

  • Honey
  • Water
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Combine the honey and water in your chosen container, whether that's a barrel, a gourd or a cavity in your cave
  2. Either leave it open to the air for a while to hopefully catch some good wild yeasts, or add your preferred yeast
  3. Leave in a warm place to bubble away until it smells nice and boozy
  4. Drink
  5. Repeat
  6. Hope you're an immortal god like Odin, or if you're not and you end up dying from liver failure try to go out fighting with a sword in your hand, then Odin might let you try some of the good stuff.
