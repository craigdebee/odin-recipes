Hot Mead

Just the thing for a cold winter's day

When Odin is with the the Icelanders this is his preferred drink, he thinks their Brennivin tastes like farts, but we won't mention "skáldfífla hlutr" (the Rhymester's share).

Ingredients

  • Mead
  • Heat

Steps

  1. Get some mead
  2. Heat it up
  3. Pour into your favourite mug or tankard
  4. Hug it with your hands
  5. Skol
  6. Drink it until you feel warm
