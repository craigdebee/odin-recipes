Mead Cocktail

Odin's Party Drink

When Odin wants to party like it's 999

Ingredients

  • Mead
  • Mead of Poetry
  • Iced Mead
  • Your preferred garnish and decoration

Steps

  1. Combine the meads in a cocktail shaker
  2. Shake while invoking Thor for a touch of thunder
  3. Pour into a large glass
  4. Get that party started
